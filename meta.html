Casting with Meta Quest allows you to share what you’re seeing in VR with others around you. You can cast to your computer, phone, or supported Chromecast device.



Quest Pro Mixed Reality Casting: We know that the Meta Quest community has asked for new ways to capture and share their awesome mixed reality experiences with this new device, so we’ll soon be adding recording and casting for MR in a future software update.

Get ready to cast

  • Download the Oculus mobile app to your phone.
  • Make sure your phone and headset are on the same Wi-Fi network and that they can directly communicate with each other.
  • Your TV or device must support Chromecast. If it's not supported, you can cast to your phone's Oculus app.

Your TV or phone must be on the same Wi-Fi network and be able to communicate directly with your headset to cast with Meta Quest.

  1. From the Oculus app, tap Menu then tap Casting.
  2. Tap on your headset then tap the device you want to cast to.

Tap Stop casting in the Oculus app when you're ready to stop sharing.

Note: If you close the Oculus app or open a different app on your phone, casting will be stopped.

Troubleshooting

If you're having issues trying to cast:

  • Restart your headset, phone and your casting device.
  • Before you begin casting, make sure your headset and the device that you’re casting to can directly communicate with each other.
  • Check for software updates on your headset, phone and your casting device.
  • Restart your Wi-Fi router.

To troubleshoot your Chromecast device,visit Google's Chromecast Help page.

Known Issues

  • Certain mesh networks and corporate or certificate-based networks may not support casting due to blocks for security purposes.These blocks are common on home guest networks, corporate and commercial networks (ex: at a hotel or airport), and some cellular tethering systems. If you can’t connect your casting device and headset to each other, please try a different network.
  • TVs with built-in Chromecast or other smart functionality may be supported, but compatibility can't be guaranteed with all models.
  • You may experience issues when casting and recording simultaneously.
  • Casting may increase battery usage or degrade in-app performance.

Voice Commands is a feature that allows you to perform different actions using your voice. This can be used for things such as navigating your settings, launching applications and resetting your headset view. Learn more about which commands you can say.

Other people in calls and apps won’t hear what you say to Voice Commands after you wake it, but they may hear you say “Hey Facebook”. Your voice will be able to be heard after you complete your voice command. You can choose to change this in Settings. Learn more about how Meta protects the privacy of your voice interactions.

  1. If you set up the Voice Commands shortcut, press twice and skip to step 4, otherwise press on your right Touch controller to pull up your universal menu.
  2. Hover over the clock on the left side of the universal menu. When Quick Settings appears, select it to open the Quick Settings panel.
  3. Select Voice Commands.
  4. You’ll be prompted to give a voice command. If you’re unsure of what to do, try “What can I say?” to see a list of supported commands.
  5. Try a voice command, like “What time is it?” or "Show me Store."

When you log in with your Facebook account, you may have the option to enable the "Hey Facebook" wake word. Enabling "Hey Facebook" will allow you to activate Voice Commands on your Meta Quest 2 device.

To opt in and begin using "Hey Facebook":
  1. Press on your right Touch controller to pull up your universal menu.
  2. Hover over the clock on the left side of the universal menu. When Quick Settings appears, select it to open the Quick Settings panel.
  3. Select Settings in the top right corner, then select Experimental
  4. Select the toggle next to "Hey Facebook" to turn this feature on.

Enabling "Hey Facebook" may affect the performance of some apps. If in-app performance is affected, "Hey Facebook" will turn off automatically and you'll receive a notification. "Hey Facebook" will still be available in Home.

Learn more about the commands you can use with “Hey Facebook”.

To choose a different voice to interact with:
  1. Press on your right Touch controller to pull up your universal menu.
  2. Hover over the clock on the left side of the universal menu. When Quick Settings appears, select it to open the Quick Settings panel.
  3. Select Settings in the top right corner, then select System.
  4. Select Voice Commands, then select Edit next to Voice Selection.
  5. Select a voice from the list to hear an example. Once you've found the voice you want to use, select Save.

A record of phrases spoken while using Voice Commands can be viewed from the Meta Quest app on your mobile phone.

To see recent voice commands:
  1. From your mobile device, open the Meta Quest app.
  2. Tap your profile picture in the top left.
  3. Tap Account Settings then tap Voice Activity.
To delete the list of commands spoken:
  1. From Voice Activity, tap Clear All.
  2. Tap OK to confirm you want to delete the list of commands.
